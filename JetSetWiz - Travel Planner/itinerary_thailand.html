Travel Itinerary

You're in Thailand


Day 1


Bangkok – Pattaya (3 Nights)

Mumbai to Mumbai tour guests will report at Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai as per the scheduled flight departure time and proceed to Bangkok. Upon arrival, Veena World Tour Manager will guide all guests for airport formalities and proceed to Pattaya. Today we visit the tallest building in Pattaya – Pattaya Tower and experience Speed Shuttle/ Sky Shuttle/ Tower Jump which gives breath-taking view of the city.

Day 2


Pattaya

Today we visit Coral Island. Here we enjoy parasailing and Speed boat ride. We shall view the colourful underwater life from the Glass Bottom Boat. We also get a chance to enjoy the various water sports like Banana Boat, water scooter, underwater sea walk etc. on your own. Later we visit Nong Nooch village – the biggest and the most beautiful botanical garden and enjoy Pottery Garden, the famous Elephant show and Thai cultural show. In the evening we enjoy the famous cabaret show – Alcazar Show.

Day 3


Pattaya

Today we visit the famous and the world’s biggest Gems Gallery where we enjoy shopping precious stones and witness a spectacular light and sound presentation of the glittering world of gems. Later we visit Frost Magical Ice Siam – get a chance to see unique ice sculptures, ice dome, an ice slide and many more activities. We then visit Art in Paradise – a unique hands-on museum based on optical illusion art which enables the human eye with the ability to trick the human brain. In the evening we relax ourselves with Scientific Thai massage.

Day 4


Pattaya – Bangkok (2 Nights)

Today we leave for Bangkok – The capital city of Thailand. Upon arrival we visit Safari World – an open zoo where visitors can marvel at the animals living in their natural habitat. At Marine Park, there are a variety of exotic marine animals and shows like Orang Utang Monkey Kick Boxing Show, Cowboy Stunt Show, Sea Lion Show, and Dolphin Show. Here we get an opportunity to have the closest encounter with the rare and endangered species of the animal kingdom. In the evening we enjoy shopping at Indra Square.

Day 5


Bangkok

Today we enjoy the city orientation tour which includes the 'Wat – Pho' – the temple of the reclining Buddha. In the evening we enjoy the dining experience along the Chao Phraya River Dinner Cruise.

Day 6


Bangkok – Departure to hometown

It's time now to say goodbye! Let's stay in touch with each other through email, phone, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram and meet again on yet another memorable tour. See you all!! Mumbai to Mumbai Tour guests will proceed to Bangkok Airport as per the scheduled group flight departure time and start their return journey back home.
