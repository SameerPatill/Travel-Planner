Travel Itinerary

You're in Andaman-Nicobar


Day 1


Port Blair (1 Night)

Tour Guests will arrive at Port Blair Airport as per their Scheduled flight. Upon arrival they will meet our tour manager at the airport and proceed to Hotel In the evening, we visit Cellular Museum and Cellular Jail, which stood mute witness to the torture meted out to the freedom fighters. History awakens as we visit 'Veer Sawarkar' Smarak and see the light and sound show. Further we also enjoy short Meet & Greet session.

Day 2


Port Blair – Havelock (Swaraj Dweep) (2 Nights)

Today we visit Kala Pani Museum, showcasing the freedom struggle and history of Andaman. Later we proceed to Havelock (Swaraj Dweep) by M V Makruzz Cruise. Swaraj Dweep is known as one of the jewels of the Andaman Islands.

Day 3


Havelock (Swaraj Dweep)

Today we visit Elephant Beach - one of the unique beaches located on Havelock Islands. Here you get a chance to enjoy various adventure water sport activities like Banana Boat, snorkelling, underwater sea walk etc. on your own. Later we we visit Radhanagar Beach - Crowned as Asia’s Best Beach and 7th best beach in the world by Time Magazine.

Day 4


Havelock (Swaraj Dweep) – Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) (1 Night)

Today we proceed to Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) by M V Makruzz Cruise. Shaheed Dweep is known for its magnificent biodiversity, unexplored coral reefs and white sandy beaches. Upon arrival, we visit Bharatpur Beach – One of the best blue water beaches in Andaman.

Day 5


Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) – Port Blair (2 Nights)

Today we proceed to Port Blair by M V Makruzz Cruise. Upon arrival, we have free time for Shopping.

Day 6


Port Blair – Baratang – Port Blair

Today we enjoy an excursion to Baratang Island (Subject to operation) The island which has dense mangrove forests and rich diversified fauna. We visit Mud volcano - India’s only active Mud Volcano. It is formed by gases and liquids excreted by the earth’s interior. There’s no lava spewed out, but mud in a semi-solid state comes out of the volcano. Later we enjoy a boat ride through the mangrove reserves to the wonderful Limestone caves, to explore stalagmite and stalactite formations which were formed millions of years ago due to the slow deposition of lime on the earth's surface.

Day 7


Port Blair – Departure

It's time now to say goodbye to our travel companions. Let's stay in touch with each other through email, phone, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram and meet again on yet another memorable tour. See you all!! Tour Guests will proceed to Port Blair airport as per their schedule flight and start their return journey back home.
