Travel Itinerary

Welcome to Coorg Ooty Mysore


Day 1


Arrive Bangalore - Coorg (290 Kms / 5 – 6 Hrs) (2 Nights)

Arrive Bangalore - Coorg (290 Kms / 5 – 6 Hrs) (2 Nights) Welcome at Bangalore and proceed towards Coorg - The Scotland of India. En route Coorg visit the famous Buddhist Monastery and the Golden Buddha Temple located in Bylekuppe. Bylekuppe - One of the largest Tibetan settlements in South India, it is known for its monasteries, handicrafts, carpet factories, and incense factory. Arrive Coorg in the evening and check into the hotel. Rest of the day free at leisure. Evening free for relaxation. Overnight in Coorg.

Day 2


Coorg Sightseeing

After breakfast, visit Dubare Elephant Camp, which is primarily an elephant-capturing and training camp of the forest department at the edge of Dubare forest. Experience White Water Rafting at Dubare Camp. Later we visit Abbey waterfalls – nestled between coffee and spice plantations, the waterfall is a combination of many streams that come together and fall into a pool of water that flows and merge into River Kaveri, Raja’s seat – it offers a breath-taking view of towering hills, green valleys, studded with paddy fields. Later we visit Madikeri Fort – is a historical landmark stands in the center of the Madikeri town. Free time for shopping at Coorg Bazar. Overnight in Coorg.

Day 3


Coorg – Mysore (140 Kms / 3 – 4 Hrs)

After breakfast, check out of the hotel and drive to Mysore. Arrive Mysore in the noon and check into the hotel. Later in the evening proceed to KRS Dam and Brindavan Gardens. One can also enjoy Sound & Light show in the Gardens. Evening free at leisure. Overnight in Mysore.

Day 4


Mysore – Ooty (140 Kms / 3 – 4 Hrs) (3 Nights)

After breakfast, check out of the hotel and visit Mysore Palace – a historical palace and the official residence of the Wadiyar dynasty who ruled the Kingdom of Mysore, Mysore Zoo – one of the oldest and most famous zoos in India and home to a wide range of species, Mysore Sand Sculpture Museum – only museum in India that is dedicated to sand sculptures and 3D Selfie Gallery. Later proceed to drive to Ooty. Overnight in Ooty.

Day 5


Ooty – Day trip to Coonoor

After breakfast, enjoy a day trip to Coonoor. In Coonoor enjoy visit to Sim’s park, Lamb’s rock and Dolphin’s nose. Return to the hotel later in the evening. Overnight in Ooty.

Day 6


Ooty Local Sightseeing

After breakfast, proceed for sightseeing at Ooty. Visit Botanical garden, Ooty lake and boat club, Dolphins Nose, Tea factory and Museum (subject to availability at the time of travel) Visit Pine forest, Pykara dam and falls. Evening back to hotel - Overnight in Ooty.

Day 7


Ooty – Coimbatore –Departure (100 Kms / 3 – 4 Hrs)

After breakfast, proceed to Coimbatore Airport to board the flight to your next destination or hometown with lots of memories to cherish for a life time!
