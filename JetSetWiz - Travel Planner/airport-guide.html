Airport Guide

Step - 1: Check-In



You can check-in for your flight online, via a mobile app, or at the airport. If you're checking in at the airport, you'll need to go to the check-in desk or kiosk and provide your travel documents (passport, visa, ID, etc.) along with your flight reservation information.

Step - 2: Baggage Drop



If you have checked-in luggage, you will need to drop it off at the designated baggage drop area.

Step - 3: Security Check



Pass through security checkpoint where you will be scanned and screened for any prohibited items.

Step - 4: Immigration Check & Customs



You may be required to show your travel documents and complete a customs declaration form if applicable.

Step - 5: Boarding



You will be called to board the flight when it is ready. Make sure to have your boarding pass and ID ready.

Step - 6: Find Your Seat



Locate your seat on the aircraft and stow your carry-on bag in the overhead bin or under the seat in front of you.
