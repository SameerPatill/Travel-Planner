Travel Itinerary

Welcome to Meghalaya


Day 1


Guwahati – Shillong (2 Nights)

Tour Guests will arrive at Guwahati Airport as per their Scheduled flight. Upon arrival they will meet our tour manager at the airport and proceed to Shillong – The capital of Meghalaya. En route we enjoy the view of beautiful Umiam Lake. In the evening all guests will have a short Meet & Greet session.

Day 2


Shillong

Today we visit Cathedral of Mary, Ward’s Lake, Lady Hydari Park and Don Bosco Museum. We then visit Elephant Fall. Later we visit the Police Bazar area and indulge in some street shopping and hit the pubs and cafes in Shillong on our own.

Day 3


Shillong – Jowai (2 Nights)

Today we proceed to Jowai. En route we also get to see fields of paddy farming. We also visit Thadlaskein Lake – a beautiful manmade Lake in Meghalaya.

Day 4


Jowai – Krang Suri – Jowai

Today we visit one of the scenic and most beautiful waterfalls in Meghalaya – Krang Suri Waterfalls.

Day 5


Jowai – Dawki – Mawlynnong – Cherrapunjee (2 Nights)

Today we head towards Dawki to see the crystal clear waters of Dawki river and enjoy boating on the river. Dawki lies at the border of India and Bangladesh and is famous for a suspension bridge over Umngot River (commonly known as Dawki bridge) constructed by British in 1932. We also visit India - Bangladesh border at Tamabil. We then proceed to Mawlynnong, Asia’s cleanest village. Mawlynnong village has been awarded as the ‘Cleanest Village in Asia’. We visit the Balancing Rock and the Living Root Bridge – Centuries old bridges grown from tangled roots. Walk around the beautiful village, you’d spot thatched khasi huts along the way, bamboo dustbins all throughout the village and every house containing beautiful gardens adorned with pretty flowers. Later we proceed to Cherrapunjee.

Day 6


Cherrapunjee

Today’s journey is full of places to admire. We start with Nongriat Trek a very scenic as well as adventurous one. You’d be crossing many suspension bridges and root bridges along the forest trail. The Double Decker Root Bridge would be the highlight. Sit by the Double Decker root bridge and soak in the raw beauty of nature. If you enjoy swimming, do not forget to bring your swim-wear with you, you will need them when you find the kid inside you unable to hold back from jumping in the turquoise blue natural pools.

Day 7


Cherrapunjee – Guwahati (1 Night)

Today morning we proceed to Guwahati. We visit Mawsmai caves, Seven Sisters Falls, and Nohkalikai Falls – the tallest plunge waterfall in India. We also visit Ram Krishna Mission. Upon arrival at Guwahati we check in to our hotels and relax.

Day 8


Guwahati – Departure to hometown

Today we visit the Kamakhya Temple – one of the 51 Shakti Peethas in the country. It’s time now to say goodbye to our travel companions. Let’s stay in touch with each other through email, phone, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram and meet again on yet another memorable tour. See you all!! Tour Guests will proceed to Guwahati airport as per their schedule flight and start their return journey back home.
