Ranka Monastery, Sikkim



Perched atop a hill, amidst the serene landscapes of Sikkim, Ranka Monastery stands as a beacon of spiritual enlightenment and tranquility. As you approach this sacred haven, you are instantly captivated by its tranquil ambiance and the aura of serenity that envelops the surroundings.

The monastery's majestic architecture and vibrant colors create a striking contrast against the backdrop of rolling hills and verdant valleys. Its pristine white walls, adorned with traditional Tibetan paintings and intricate motifs, exude an air of timeless beauty and devotion.

Ranka Monastery is a sanctuary for the soul, a place where the mind finds solace and the heart finds serenity. It is an invitation to explore the depths of spirituality and embrace the profound wisdom of ancient traditions.

Banjhakri Waterfall, Pirdi



Banjhakri Waterfall, nestled amidst the lush greenery of Sikkim, is a mesmerizing natural wonder that captivates the senses and transports you to a realm of enchantment. As you approach this cascading marvel, you are greeted by the symphony of rushing water and the cool mist that rejuvenates your spirit.

Banjhakri Waterfall, derived from the Nepali word "Banjhakri" meaning "forest shaman," holds a mystical allure that resonates with ancient legends and folklore. Surrounded by a dense forest, the waterfall exudes a tranquil energy that invites you to reconnect with nature and tap into the depths of your own inner being.

Whether you are seeking solace, adventure, or simply a moment of respite from the chaos of everyday life, Banjhakri Waterfall offers a sanctuary that rejuvenates the mind, body, and soul.

Tsomogo Lake, Sikkim



Nestled like a sparkling jewel in the heart of the Himalayas, Tsomgo Lake is a mesmerizing masterpiece of nature that beckons adventurers and nature lovers alike. As you venture towards this pristine lake, you are greeted by the awe-inspiring beauty that unfolds before your eyes.

Tsomgo Lake, also known as Changu Lake, boasts crystal-clear turquoise waters that mirror the surrounding snow-capped peaks and the cerulean sky above. The lake's tranquil surface shimmers with a captivating brilliance, creating a picture-perfect reflection that seems almost surreal.

Tsomgo Lake is a testament to the wonders of Mother Nature, a place where beauty and tranquility converge in perfect harmony.

Tea Estates, Darjeeling



Hidden amidst the rugged terrain of the Himalayas, the Sissu Waterfall is a mesmerizing natural spectacle that captures the essence of untamed beauty. As you venture into the enchanting village of Sissu, you are greeted by the thunderous roar of cascading water, drawing you closer to its awe-inspiring presence.

Surrounded by lush greenery and towering mountains, the waterfall creates a serene oasis in the midst of the rugged landscape. The air is filled with the invigorating freshness of the mountains, as the waterfall's mist gently caresses your skin, invigorating your senses and rejuvenating your spirit.

The surrounding area offers a perfect vantage point to admire the waterfall's splendor. You can find a tranquil spot along the banks of the river, sit back, and soak in the breathtaking views. The verdant landscape, the towering cliffs, and the sound of rushing water create a picturesque tableau that feels straight out of a postcard.
